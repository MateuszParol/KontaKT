## Lessons Learned
- `ttk.Treeview` provides significantly better performance for large lists in Tkinter applications compared to complex compositions of `customtkinter` frames and labels.
- Modal selection windows offer a much more scalable and user-friendly experience for selecting related records (like Contractors and Accounts) than traditional Comboboxes when dealing with hundreds or thousands of options.
- The Peewee ORM pairs well with CustomTkinter for rapid desktop app development.
- A background training thread for the AI engine prevents UI blocking and allows "live" learning loops.
